ToolSeq is a bundle of useful, high quality plugins and scripts pack which has 16 tools that can simplify and reinforce 3D modeling pipeline for Autodesk Maya.
They are written with Python and Qt for the best maintainability, flexibility and version compatibility. Bottleneck parts are implemented with OpenMaya SDK, other parts are implemented with maya_cmds. All functionality of tools are compatible with internal Maya Undo and Redo stack.
Users are supported by providing 23 tutorial videos including subtitles that have 2 hours 31 minutes length.
It is selected by Autodesk as one of Featured Apps at 2024.
