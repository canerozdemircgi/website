ToolSeq is a bundle of high quality plugins and scripts, which includes 16 tools, that can simplify and reinforce the 3D modeling pipeline for Autodesk Maya.
The suite is written in Python and Qt for flexibility, version compatibility and maintainability. It utilizes maya.cmds while bottleneck sections are implemented with OpenMaya SDK.
All functionality is compatible with Undo and Redo stack.
Users are supported by 23 tutorial videos with provided subtitles, offering 2 hours 31 minutes of content.
ToolSeq is available on Autodesk Store and Highend3D. It was selected by Autodesk as a Featured App in 2024.
